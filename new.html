Welcome to RAC HSS Katameri

RAC HSS Katameri has been a beacon of knowledge in the Kozhikode district, providing quality education to thousands of students. Our school is committed to fostering academic excellence and moral values.

We consist of Grades 8 to 12. As a Private Aided institution, we provide a nurturing environment with a perfect balance of traditional values and modern learning techniques.
