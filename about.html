Institutional Profile

Established in 1983, RAC Higher Secondary School, Katameri is a distinguished Private Aided institution located in the rural landscape of the Thodannur block, Kozhikode. The school serves as a cornerstone of education for the region, offering Malayalam-medium instruction for students from Grades 8 through 12.

Facilities & Resources

Academic Infrastructure: With around 50 functional classrooms in excellent condition, we provide a spacious and conducive learning environment for our students.
Technology: Our dedicated Computer Aided Learning (CAL) lab features 25 fully functional computers for digital literacy.
Hygiene & Water: We ensure health and safety with a functional well-water system, 10 boys' toilets, and 15 girls' toilets.
Welfare: Freshly prepared mid-day meals are provided daily on the school premises.

The school is easily approachable by all-weather roads and maintains a vast playground and a library to support holistic development.
